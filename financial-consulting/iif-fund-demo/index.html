                                                                        After you submit a request

                                                                        Outline only — timelines and obligations follow contracts and regulations.

                                                                        Website submissions are not approvals. A typical institutional path looks like this:

                                                                        1. Acknowledgement We confirm receipt when contact details are valid; automated messages may still require human triage.
                                                                        2. KYC / AML checks Where a mandate applies, suitability and documentation are completed outside anonymous browsing.
                                                                        3. Mandate review Risk, structure, and regulatory fit are assessed — timelines vary by complexity and jurisdiction.
                                                                        4. Outcome You receive a substantive response or a scoped next step — or a decline without obligation to finance.

                                                                        This is a general description only. Binding process is defined in Terms and in executed agreements.
