Privacy policy (website)

Public informational site — not legal advice. For institutional data handling, refer to contracts, KYC flows, and Terms on the main site.

1. What this site stores on your device

  • Browser storage (localStorage): language preference, optional login/session flags, profile fields you save in the dashboard, and similar UI state. This stays on your device unless you clear site data.
  • Cookies: third-party translation (Google Translate widget, when enabled) may set cookies according to Google’s policies.

2. Third-party services & outbound links

  • Search: “Search engine” opens external SearXNG instances; “Google search” opens Google. Queries are sent to those providers under their terms.
  • Maps, news tickers, APIs: embedded or linked resources (e.g. market news, geolocation helpers) are loaded from their respective domains.
  • Serverless proxy (optional): analysis features may call a configured HTTPS proxy (e.g. on Vercel) which forwards search requests to SearXNG — see site operations documentation.

3. Your choices

  • Clear cookies and site data for this origin in your browser settings.
  • Avoid signing in or saving profile data if you do not want it stored locally.
  • Do not use third-party search links if you prefer not to send queries externally.

4. Contact

Use the Contact section on the main page for institutional enquiries.
