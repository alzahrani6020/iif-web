Privacy policy (website)

Last updated with site releases — not legal advice. Institutional processing under KYC/contracts is governed by those instruments and the main Terms.

1. Data we do not centrally collect through this static site alone

GitHub Pages serves static files. The Fund does not automatically receive your localStorage contents or device files simply because you opened the page. Data appears to the Fund when you submit forms, email, or use connected backends described in each flow.

2. What may be stored on your device

  • localStorage: language choice (iif-lang), login/session flags, profile fields you save, certificate image preferences, analytics-style click IDs if enabled — all remain on your browser until cleared.
  • Session storage: may be used for short-lived UI state in some flows.
  • Cookies: mainly from third parties when you use embedded tools (e.g. Google Translate).

4. Your controls

  • Clear site data for this origin in browser settings.
  • Use private/incognito mode for a session without persisting prior localStorage.
  • Avoid third-party search buttons if you do not want queries sent externally.

5. Children

This site is oriented to institutions and professional users. If you believe a minor’s data was submitted in error, contact us via Contact to request deletion where applicable.

6. Changes

We may update this page when tools or hosting change. Track privacy.html in your site’s source repository for revision history.
