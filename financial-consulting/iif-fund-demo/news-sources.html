News & market ticker sources

Headlines are fetched from public RSS feeds. The Fund does not control third-party content. Links open external sites.

Global strip (static links)

Shortcuts in the top strip (Reuters, Bloomberg, CNBC, central banks, etc.) are normal hyperlinks, not automated feeds.

Asian & Arab strips (API: /api/news)

Typical sources include (subject to change in api/news.js):

  • BBC News (world, Asia, Middle East, Arabic)
  • Straits Times, Caixin (caixin.com RSS), Japan Times, CNBC
  • Al Jazeera (Arabic site + English RSS), Channel NewsAsia (UAE feeds)

Optional local translation for Arabic UI: set IIF_TICKER_TRANSLATE_URL to your NLLB service (same contract as /api/translate).

مصادر موجز الأسواق
